Mince Meat balls

Description

Meat balls are available all around the world, this is a recipe for the austrian version of meat balls.

Ingredients

  • 1kg mixed minced meat
  • old bread rolls
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Cut the old bread rolls in pieces adn put them in water to get them soaked
  2. Cook the onions until they are glzing
  3. Mix the bread, onions and spices with the minced meat
  4. Form roughly hand size balls from this mass
  5. Cook them in a pan with oil
  6. Enjoy!
